ShinRa Department of Public Safety

Welcome to the Department of Public Safety, home of the ShinRa military. Public safety is an important part of maintaining a high functioning company, and covers a broad range of services.

ShinRa Army

The ShinRa army is the strongest military force in all of Gaia. Using the latest equipment and technology, they are able to accomplish any task they take on. The ShinRa army also serves as mercenaries for hire. If you are interested, visit our contact page and provide the details of your request.

SOLDIER

SOLDIER is the elite unity from the ShinRa army. Not only were they the top members of the infantry, but they have undergone mako therapy to further their combat prowess. These elite members are ranked by 3rd class, 2nd class, and the exclusive 1st class, which historically has only taken a select few. The legendary warrior Sephiroth belonged to SOLDIER 1st class.

Investigative Division of General Affairs

This small department contains just four members. Their jobs are to seek out potential SOLDIER recruits, but also take rigorous combat training and serve as bodyguards to ShinRa president. They certainly do not commit illicit acts of violence or serve as assassins to protect company secrets.
