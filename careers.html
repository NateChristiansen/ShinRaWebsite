ShinRa Scientist

Scientific Research

Professor Hojo

As a ShinRa scientist, you will conduct scientific experiments and research, which definitely does not include human genetic experiments or immoral studies. Under Professor Hojo, you will develop the future of ShinRa technology.

Develop the latest in mako and robotic technology.
Perform scientific research and document findings.
Engineer new technology utilizing research subjects.
Maintain a strong moral and ethical workspace.
Perform absolutely no genetic modification or human cloning.

Must have graduated with a PhD in a major scientific field.
Must have passion for research and new technology.
Must think outside the box in order to push ShinRa in new directions.
Must not be willing to genetically engineer a perfect SOLDIER operative that will destroy the world and become a god.
