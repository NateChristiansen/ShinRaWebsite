ShinRa Power Electric Company

Your friendly electric company providing services to Midgar

What we do:

We are ShinRa, and we provide services to Midgar and other surrounding cities. Our mako reactors generate electricity that provides power to the developed world. We price ourselves on upholding strong ethical policies and maintaining our strong operation standards.

Our departments all operate under high conditions and keep optimal safety regulations up to date. We perform rigorous health inspects every 6 months. We also do thorough testing and modeling before conducting any manufacturing of weapons to ensure that accidents are minimized and the entire process runs smoothly.

Our History

We started as a weapons development company that discovered how to harvest the lifestream. This energy is siphoned into reactors which then turns into mako. Mako is what powers your homes and devices. After discovering this, we built the city of Midgar around our headquarters. This city quickly became the most technologically advanced city in all of Gaia.

Ecological concerns

Mako energy is completely harmless for the planet. The lifestream we harvest and refine becomes the mako that you consume. The mako that gets consumed naturally returns to the planet. We have performed extensive scientific research into this matter and all of our scientists have come to the same conclusion, that mako energy is a reliable green energy that does no harm to Gaia.
